# Add Messenger Product - Direct Methods

## Can't Find "Add Product"? Try These Methods:

---

## Method 1: Direct URL (Easiest!)

1. **Copy this URL and replace `YOUR_APP_ID` with your App ID:**
   ```
   https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/dashboard/
   ```

2. **To find your App ID:**
   - Look at the current page URL in your browser
   - It should look like: `https://developers.facebook.com/apps/123456789/dashboard/`
   - The number `123456789` is your App ID

3. **Then try this direct Messenger setup URL:**
   ```
   https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/messenger/settings/
   ```
   
   Replace `YOUR_APP_ID` with your actual App ID number!

---

## Method 2: Via "Use Cases" Section

Since you added "Facebook Login" as a use case earlier, try:

1. **In the left sidebar**, click on **"Use cases"**
2. **Look for:** 
   - A section about adding more use cases
   - An "Add use case" button
   - Or look for "Messenger" or "Business messaging" options

3. **Click to add Messenger/Business messaging**

---

## Method 3: Check "Required actions" Section

1. **In the left sidebar**, click on **"Required actions"**
2. **Look for** any mention of products or Messenger
3. There might be a link or button to add products

---

## Method 4: Look at Top Navigation Bar

1. **At the very top**, look for navigation menu items:
   - "Docs"
   - "Tools" 
   - "Support"
   - "Apps"

## Method 5: Check the Main Dashboard Content

1. **Look at the CENTER of the page** (main content area)
2. **Scroll all the way down** - sometimes "Add Product" is at the bottom
3. **Look for**:
   - "Get started" section
   - "Integrations" section
   - "Available products" section
   - Any blue or green buttons

---

## Method 6: Direct Products Page

Try this URL pattern:

```
https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/products/
```

Replace `YOUR_APP_ID` with your App ID!

---

## Method 7: Via Settings

1. **Click on "Basic"** under "App settings" (in left sidebar)
2. **Look for** a "Products" tab or section
3. **Or click on "Advanced"** â†’ Look for product-related options

## What's Your App ID?

**To find it:**
1. Look at your browser's address bar (URL)
2. It should show something like: `developers.facebook.com/apps/123456789/`
3. The number `123456789` is your App ID

**Once you have it, try this direct link:**
```
https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/messenger/settings/
```

Replace `YOUR_APP_ID` with your actual number!
