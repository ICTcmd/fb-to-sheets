## üîç Look for "Add Product" or "Products" Section

Since Messenger isn't in your sidebar yet, you need to add it:

### Option 1: Main Dashboard Area (Center)

1. **Look at the main content area (center of the screen)**
   - Scroll up or down if needed
   - Look for:
     - **"Add Product"** button
     - **"+ Add Product"** button
     - A **"Products"** section
     - **"Get Started"** or **"Add Integration"** button

2. **Click on it** ‚Üí Find "Messenger" in the list ‚Üí Click "Set Up"

---

### Option 2: Left Sidebar - Look Above or Below App Settings

1. **Scroll UP in the left sidebar** (above "App settings")
   - Look for:
     - **"Products"** section
     - **"Add Product"** link
     - Any other sections

2. **Or scroll DOWN in the left sidebar** (below "Alert Inbox")
   - Look for product-related items

---

### Option 3: Top Navigation Bar

1. **Look at the top navigation bar** (where "Docs", "Tools", "Support" are)
   - Click on **"Apps"** dropdown
   - Or look for a **"Products"** or **"Add Product"** option

---

### Option 4: Use Search Bar

1. **Click the search bar** at the top right
2. **Type:** "add product" or "messenger"
3. **Click on relevant results**

---

### Option 5: Go Back to Main Products Page

1. **In the top left**, click **"Apps"** ‚Üí **"App Dashboard"**
2. **Or go to:** https://developers.facebook.com/apps
3. **Click on your app name**
4. **Look for a "Products" or "Add Product" section**
