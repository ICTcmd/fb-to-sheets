## Quick Methods to Get to Messenger Settings:

### Method 1: Use Direct URL (FASTEST!)

1. **Get your App ID** from your browser URL (the number)
   - Look at the address bar
   - Find the number like: `/apps/123456789/`

2. **Copy and paste this URL** (replace `YOUR_APP_ID` with your number):
   ```
   https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/messenger/settings/
   ```

3. **Press Enter** - This should take you directly to Messenger Settings!

---

## What You'll See at Messenger Settings:

Once you get there, you should see:

**1. Access Tokens Section:**
- Dropdown to select your Facebook Page
- "Generate Token" button
- Your Page Access Token (after generating)

**2. Webhooks Section:**
- "Add Callback URL" or "Edit" button
- Callback URL field
- Verify Token field
- Subscribe to events checkboxes
