# Messenger Configuration - Get to Settings

## ‚úÖ You're in the Right Place!

You're looking at the Messenger permissions and features. Now you need to get to the **Messenger Settings** page where you can:
1. Get your **Page Access Token**
2. Set up **Webhooks**

---

## How to Get to Messenger Settings:

### Option 1: Look for Settings Tab/Menu

1. **In this page**, look for tabs or menu items at the top:
   - "Permissions and features" (you're here)
   - "Settings" tab ‚Üê Click this!
   - "Webhooks" tab
   - "Access Tokens" tab

2. **Click on "Settings" or "Webhooks"** to get to the configuration page

---

### Option 2: Look in Left Sidebar

1. **In the left sidebar**, look for:
   - **"Messenger"** or **"Business messaging"** 
   - Under "Messenger", you might see:
     - Settings
     - Webhooks
     - Access Tokens

2. **Click on "Messenger"** in the sidebar, then navigate to "Settings" or "Webhooks"

---

### Option 3: Use URL (Direct Method)

1. **Get your App ID** from the browser URL (the number)
2. **Try this URL:**
   ```
   https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/messenger/settings/
   ```
   Replace `YOUR_APP_ID` with your App ID

3. **Or try:**
   ```
   https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/messenger/webhooks/
   ```

---

### Option 4: Back to Dashboard, Then Navigate

1. **Click "Dashboard"** in the left sidebar
2. **In the main dashboard**, look for:
   - "Messenger" section
   - Or click on "Engage with customers on Messenger" again
   - Look for tabs like: "Settings", "Webhooks", "Access Tokens"

---

## What You're Looking For:

Once you get to Messenger Settings, you should see:

**1. Access Tokens Section:**
- "Generate Token" button
- Dropdown to select your Page
- Your Page Access Token (after generating)

**2. Webhooks Section:**
- "Add Callback URL" or "Edit" button
- Where to enter your webhook URL
- Where to enter Verify Token
- Where to subscribe to events
